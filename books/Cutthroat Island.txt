Cutthroat Island is a 1995 romantic comedy adventure film directed by Renny Harlin and written by Robert King and Marc Norman based on a story by Michael Frost Beckner, James Gorman, Bruce A. Evans, and Raymond Gideon. It stars Geena Davis, Matthew Modine, and Frank Langella.[4] The film is an international co-production among companies in the United States, France, Germany, and Italy.

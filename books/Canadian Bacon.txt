Canadian Bacon is a 1995 American comedy film written, produced, and directed by Michael Moore which satirizes Canada¨CUnited States relations along the Canada¨CUnited States border. The film stars an ensemble cast featuring Alan Alda, John Candy, Bill Nunn, Kevin J. O'Connor, Rhea Perlman, Kevin Pollak, G.D. Spradlin, and Rip Torn.

Lightning Jack is a 1994 American-Australian Western comedy film directed by Simon Wincer and written by Paul Hogan. Hogan stars as "Lightning" Jack Kane, a fading Australian gun fighter and bank robber in the American frontier. Jack seeks to be recognized among the most notorious outlaws as he is ignored or dismissed by newspapers and lawmen and the United States moves into the 20th century. Cuba Gooding Jr. plays Ben, a mute shopkeeper who wants to become an outlaw, and teams up with Jack, who serves as Ben's mentor as they rob banks. Beverly D'Angelo plays Lana, a Western saloon hooker with a heart of gold who is in love with Jack.

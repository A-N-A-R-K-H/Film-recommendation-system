The NeverEnding Story III: Escape from Fantasia (also known as: The NeverEnding Story III: Return to Fantasia) is a 1994 film, the second sequel to the fantasy film The NeverEnding Story (following the first sequel, The NeverEnding Story II: The Next Chapter).[1] It starred Jason James Richter as the Bastian Bux, and Jack Black in one of his early roles as the school bully, Slip. This film used the characters from Michael Ende's novel The Neverending Story (1979), but introduced a new storyline.

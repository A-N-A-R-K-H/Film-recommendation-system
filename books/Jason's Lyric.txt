Jason's Lyric is a 1994 erotic romantic drama film, written by Bobby Smith, Jr, directed by Doug McHenry, who co-produced the film with George Jackson and Marilla Lane Ross, and starring Allen Payne, Jada Pinkett Smith, Bokeem Woodbine, Treach, Eddie Griffin, Lahmard Tate, Lisa Nicole Carson, and Forest Whitaker. Set in Houston's Third Ward, the film is a story about misunderstood young American adults learning how to deal with love and maturity.

The Cable Guy is a 1996 American thriller comedy film directed by Ben Stiller, starring Jim Carrey and Matthew Broderick.[4] It was released in the United States on June 14, 1996. The film co stars Leslie Mann, Jack Black, George Segal, Diane Baker, Eric Roberts, Owen Wilson, Janeane Garofalo, David Cross, Andy Dick, Amy Stiller, and Bob Odenkirk.[4]

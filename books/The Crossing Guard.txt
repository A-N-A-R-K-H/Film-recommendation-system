The Crossing Guard is a 1995 American independent thriller drama film co-produced, written and directed by American actor Sean Penn. The film tells the story of Freddy Gale, a man who has been tormented for more than five years following his daughter's death in a car accident. Since the tragedy, he became an alcoholic, ruining his marriage, and when he finds out that the man who was responsible for the death of his daughter is being released from prison, he decides to set out for vengeance. The film stars Jack Nicholson, David Morse, Robin Wright and Anjelica Huston.

The Woman in Question (released in the United States as Five Angles on Murder) is a 1950 British mystery film directed by Anthony Asquith and starring Jean Kent, Dirk Bogarde and John McCallum.[1] After a woman is murdered, the complex and very different ways in which she is seen by several people are examined.[2] It was loosely adapted into the 1954 Indian film Andha Naal.[3]

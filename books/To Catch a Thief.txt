To Catch a Thief is a 1955 American romantic thriller film directed by Alfred Hitchcock, from a screenplay by John Michael Hayes based on the 1952 novel To Catch a Thief by David Dodge.[2] The film stars Cary Grant[2] as a retired cat burglar[2] who has to save his reformed reputation by catching an imposter preying on the wealthy tourists of the French Riviera. Grace Kelly[2] stars opposite him as his romantic interest in her final film with Hitchcock.
